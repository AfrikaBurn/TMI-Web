The Wave

2016-10-28 23:27
R40000ZAR (South African Rands)
The Wave We would like to build a 6-8ft wave, which is going to be gorgeous to look at, and even more spectacular when burnt. At night, beautfully lit. People will walk through the wave, and feel the ocean. And hear it!! The strength of the this project is its simplicity. See 3D image. I am a surfer. Life is a wave. You ride it as best you can. Sometimes you get dumped. But you come for air, and get back out and catch the next one! Hopefully this time a beautiful ride. Surfing is playing. A wave is God's gift to us to PLAY with :) Medium

Simple design, strong and sturdy

Ocean sounds. Waves crashing, whales calling, dolphins, bird life. A time to reflect on the ocean. Deep in the desert. Grounding, inspiring and uplifting.
Strong, every single piece reinforces the other. Mimicking nature, strong and simple.
A Wave is like life. Ephemeral. It comes it, goes. We play. And we let go. A beautiful cycle.
I have a good track record of getting things done. And having fun along the way. It would be wonderful to share this experience with fellow Burners.
