BLOW ME

2016-11-06 16:01
Artwork Registration
inflatable dancing bodies and creatures - shaped hot air balloons fixed to small simple structures on the ground. creating shade and shapes by day, and great glowing spectacles by night. Our idea is to create some fantastical creatures that reference the environment surrounding the burn, as well as the icons and shapes of the burn itself - ie the running figures of the clan, the beasts and energies that once roamed those plains, swam in the oceans that once covered them and soared through the skies above them (and may - one day again) We want to make them as massive as we possibly can, and invite interaction. The works could be puppeteered via lines and walked across the binnekring. The works are large, but light - can be easily moved (with some vehicular support) and deployed in different areas. Ideally we will utilise solar power, and wind, but generators - (would like to avoid) and fires or gas (ala hot air balloons )would also work. We still need to do tests and see what works best at the scale we hope to achieve. We are also looking into pumping them with human effort, squooshing air from big cushions that are inflated by waving (like the blow-up couches) into the inlets. The project was first inspired by street art seen in new york - http://www.downvids.net/street-art-inflatable-bag-monsters--31476.html, also those crazy things you see at tyre shops. (The visual references are given in the absence of drawings and designs at this time, only to reflect techniques and the concept and no way reflect the final design that will be applied - we beg your indulgence here - we will submit drawings and designs soon) We aim to get some crazy comical movement, as well as dreamy evocativeshapes that create shade and a place to gather in the day, and elevated glowing, moving spectacles that can be projected on at night. We recently made some inflatable costumes that reminded us again of this dream project. We have access to high quality zero porosity parachute fabric that will ensure that they last for some years. Ideally we will solar power them, but generators and fires or gas (ala hot air balloons) could also work. We still need to do tests and see what works best at the scale we hope to achieve.
ideas are still brewing
Child Focused, Circus Arts, Dancers, DJ's, Fire Dances / Performers, Illusion, Magic, Mime, Performance Art, Puppetry

D'lightfull Tower

2016-10-07 21:00
Artwork Registration
The D'lightfull Tower is a ten meter high timber structure which is designed to BURN. Inspired by that other iconic tower in a romantic French city and the Makondo family trees, this tower is a melding of European and African cultures. During the day, the space beneath the tower will be a hive of activity, currently planned are a soup kitchen day, massage day and kids playground day etc. At night the tower will be lit from within, with the crowning gem being a colourful radiation of glory.
We welcome any and all performers to enhance our space!

Please Wave

2016-09-27 16:06
Artwork Registration
It is a 15ft wave emerging from the dust of the Tankwa. It is 4,8m high, 20m long and 7.2m deep and is constructed from unpainted plywood and timber.cladding We would like to project images onto the wave surface at night and have mutant vehicles come and PLAY with us. Positioned correctly this space will create a wind free PLAY space for other artists and fellow burners. It will be an absolute vision of beauty on the horizon of the Tankwa. During the day we will introduce and element of FUN and provide great photographic opportunities and MANY CLASSIC MOMENTS It will be built to BURN..
if positioned correctly it will provide a great wind free PLAY space for any artist wanting to perform acoustically. The unpainted wave face will create a perfect screen for films or mapping shows.

Sandy Bay XL (Working Title)

2016-10-27 12:44
Artwork Registration
Our project is an oversized beach scene. To bring everyone back to childhood where they had to climb up onto beach chairs, and beach balls were bigger than they were. There will be a wooden beach umbrella with material shade that is 5m in diameter and 3m off the ground (Picture the size of a 5x5m room with a 3m ceiling). Two wooden beach chairs where your feet don't even touch the ground that 2 people can sit on together. As well as some other oversized beach games.
Basically open to anyone who asks... band could totally perform under the shade of the umbrella

Window Shopping

2016-11-08 01:02
Artwork Registration
Every year at Afrikaburn you always say to your friends lets go........ and you head off to the next best theme camp, 2017 will be the year you instruct your friends to go 'window shopping'. Window shopping will be a window to look at, in and pose in front of, but not only will it be a shop window it will be the Transit Lounge window shopping experience. While waiting at the Transit Lounge you will be able to window shop cause at the Transit Lounge window we offer specials like 'gifting' not sales.live mannequins and photo opportunities while waiting for any connecting individual departing from Tankwa while indulging in music dance and a fabulous cocktail hosted by our international crazy bound crew. We are interactive and always delayed but never without surprise

Wordplay

2016-11-06 10:43
Artwork Registration
This year I want to really work with the theme "play" by creating an installation thar is made out of children's play blocks. The playblocks will be large scale though. They will be made out of cardboard boxes that I will get from the scrapyard the bottom ones and strategic ones will be filled with stacked newspaper and scrap wood. To weigh them down and make a solid construction. Besides the weigh we will sink poles through the boxes at strategic points. Boxes will be painted in different colours and have letters to look like play blocks. Because playblocks have educational value the letters will spell out in cross-puzzle fashion the 11 principles of Afrikaburn. Aimed at creating awareness with burners as to what it's really about. Each one teach one is what I'm doing. Besides teaching the principles I will teach by example by using aware materials..cardboard box, newspaper and invader plants to go with the environmental theme. On the inside I would like to use the word " play" in the sense of theatre. We do daily performances and I want to create a bit of a classical theatre atmosphere. Thus really play with the word play...wordplay is childsplay! Let's play!
We do a performance once a day. But it would be great if the space is used in the interest of burners and the burn. If other live musicians come in I can also paint with them. Anything that will be enjoyable and entertaining for burners

Yggdrasil The Tree of Life

2016-11-06 17:36
Artwork Registration
Yggdrasil, the giant baobab Tree of Life, is 35 meters high and 50m in circumference. The name Yggdrasil comes from the giant mythical tree of the Norse. This is a marriage of all people North, South, East and West. Yggdrasil will be made of thousands of parts of different ideas collected from children and adults of different cultures and abilities. Among the branches of the tree there is a viking ship resembling the bringing of dreams to life by imagining the impossible possible. It's made of wood and will have interactive secret areas hidden in it roots and belly. You can dance, play and pray in the tree. It is alive and changing depending one who is there and what time of day it is. You can follow the spiral staircase up to the the ship pausing on various balconies and hidden magical areas, eventually arriving at the top where you can send your dreams out on the winds of the Tankwa 20m above the ground.
There will be lots of different areas in this space that will lend itself to small intimate performances. We encourage people to become the magic, the living legend of the tree. Follow this instinct, trust their intuition, and have the courage to set all their inhibitions free. The open space in the lower part of the tree is a perfect place for performances. People can sit and watch all around and from the second and third levels of the tree.
Child Focused, Circus Arts, Dancers, Fire Dances / Performers, Illusion, Live Music, Magic, Mime, Performance Art, Puppetry, Spoken Word, Theatre
